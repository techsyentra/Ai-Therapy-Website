Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join as a therapist?
You can start by clicking "Signup" or "Login" to create an account. You'll need to provide your professional credentials to be verified on our platform.
Is the platform HIPAA compliant?
Yes, our platform adheres to strict HIPAA guidelines. All interactions are secure, and no patient identifiers are permanently stored or exposed outside of active sessions.
How are sessions conducted?
Sessions are mediated by the AI assistant under the direct supervision of a licensed psychotherapist. The therapist remains in full control at all times, guiding the AI interaction.
Can I book a demo?
Absolutely! Fill out the contact form above specifically mentioning "Demo", and our team will get in touch to schedule a personalized walkthrough.
