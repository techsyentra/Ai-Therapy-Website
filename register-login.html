Terms of Agreement

1. This platform is intended solely for licensed mental health professionals and other approved professional users. 2. Access is subject to account review, licensure verification, and approval by the platform administrator. 3. The platform is designed as an AI reflective tool used under live professional supervision. It is not a substitute for independent clinical judgment. 4. Users agree not to enter protected health information or identifying patient information unless and until the platform is specifically configured and approved for compliant handling of such data. 5. The platform owner may suspend, deny, or revoke access for incomplete registration, failed verification, suspected misuse, inaccurate submissions, or breach of platform rules. 6. By registering, the user certifies that submitted information is accurate and belongs to the registering professional. 7. Additional legal, privacy, and professional-use terms may apply and should be reviewed before activation.

Professional Use Agreement

1. The registrant represents that they are applying for access in a professional capacity. 2. The registrant agrees that use of the platform will remain within the scope of lawful and ethical professional practice. 3. The registrant agrees that the platform will be used only as permitted by applicable law, licensure rules, supervision structure, and platform policies. 4. The registrant understands that registration does not guarantee approval, activation, or continued access. 5. The registrant agrees not to share credentials and not to permit unauthorized persons to access the platform. 6. The registrant agrees that uploaded licensure documents may be reviewed for identity and professional verification purposes. 7. The registrant agrees to comply with all future administrative requests reasonably necessary to verify professional eligibility.
