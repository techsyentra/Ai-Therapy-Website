Platform Overview

Professional Support for Licensed Psychotherapists

Psychotherapy Now provides a structured AI-supported environment designed to assist licensed psychotherapists in their practice. The platform focuses on enhancing therapeutic discovery and awareness through reflective AI interaction, while maintaining the clinician's absolute authority over the session.

The platform is built on modern principles of psychological support, integrating structured dialogue modes that therapists can guide in real-time. It is designed to be a tool for professional growth and session enhancement.
