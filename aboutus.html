About Us

A Therapist-Directed AI Session Platform

Psychotherapy Now provides an environment where the therapist directs and supervises AI-assisted interaction during sessions. The therapist remains fully responsible for clinical decisions, guidance, and session direction, while the system functions strictly as a controlled tool within the therapeutic process to increase awareness and support behavioral change

  • Therapist-Supervised AI Interaction
  • Licensed Psychotherapists Only
  • Therapist Maintains Full Session Authority
  • Secure, Privacy-Aware Digital Environment

  • Supervised AI-Assisted Sessions

    Conduct 15 to 30 minute sessions in which the AI interacts with the patient while the therapist maintains real-time control over the AI approach throughout the session.

  • Secure & HIPAA-Compliant Environment

    All sessions operate in encrypted audio rooms with clinician access. HIPAA compliant safeguards, strict privacy controls, and license verification create a protected, ethical space for reliable and focused clinical work.

  • Professional Support Framework

    The website is constructed to help the therapist approach the client from perspectives that include techniques based on the integration of the most utilized therapies of the last few decades. Techniques derived from Gestalt Therapy, NLP, CBT, Ericksonian Therapy and Behavioral Therapies and Dr. Kogen's 50 years of training and experience have been systematically included in the AI programming instructions.

Who We Are

We are a forward-thinking digital health platform focused on empowering licensed psychotherapists with advanced AI tools. Our mission is to bridge human expertise with intelligent systems to integrate therapy sessions.

Our Mission

To provide a secure and structured AI-assisted environment where therapists can guide meaningful patient interactions while maintaining full control of the therapeutic process.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in AI-supported psychotherapy, improving mental health outcomes through innovation, ethics, and professional supervision.
