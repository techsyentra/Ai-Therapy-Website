Required Documents

Please prepare all of the following documents before beginning your registration.

Professional License

A valid, current license issued by your state or national licensing board confirming your authorization to practice psychotherapy.

Required

Degree Certificate

Accredited degree in Psychology, Counseling, Psychiatry, or related mental health field from a recognized institution.

Required

Government-Issued ID

Passport, national identity card, or official government-issued photo identification for identity verification.

Required

NPI Number

National Provider Identifier for practitioners registered in the United States. International therapists may provide local equivalent.

Required

Practice Registration

Proof of active practice registration confirming your current status as a licensed, practicing psychotherapist.

Recommended

Professional Photo

A high-quality professional headshot for your therapist profile. Clear face, neutral background preferred.

Optional

Document Submission Guidelines

Accepted Formats

PDF documents (preferred) — max 10MB per file
High-resolution JPEG or PNG images (min 300dpi)
Documents must be clearly legible and fully visible
All documents must be in English or officially translated
Licenses must show expiry date and be currently valid
Government ID must show full legal name and photo

Not Accepted

Blurry, cropped, or partially visible documents
Expired licenses or outdated credentials
Screenshots or photos of screens
Documents belonging to another individual
Unverified or self-certified credentials
Files larger than 10MB or unsupported formats

Important: Data Privacy & Security

All submitted documents are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption and stored in HIPAA-compliant servers. Your credentials are reviewed only by our certified compliance team and are never shared with third parties. Documents are permanently deleted after verification is complete unless legally required to be retained.
