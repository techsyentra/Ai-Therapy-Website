PSYCHOTHERAPY NOW

A professional interface for Licensed Psychotherapists, designed to increase awareness and behavioral change through structured, therapist-supervised dialogue.

  • Supervised AI-Assisted Sessions

    Conduct structured 15–45 minute sessions where the AI interacts with your patient, while you maintain real-time control to guide or adjust the AI’s approach throughout the session.

  • Secure & HIPAA-Compliant Environment

    All sessions operate in encrypted audio rooms with clinician access. HIPAA compliant safeguards, strict privacy controls, and license verification create a protected, ethical space for reliable and focused clinical work.

  • Professional Support Framework

    The website is constructed to help the therapist approach the client from perspectives that include techniques based on the integration of the most utilized therapies of the last few decades. Techniques derived from Gestalt Therapy, NLP, CBT, Ericksonian Therapy and Behavioral Therapies and Dr. Kogen's 50 years of training and experience have been systematically included in the AI programming instructions.

A Therapist-Directed AI Session Platform

Psychotherapy Now provides a structured environment where the therapist directs and supervises AI-assisted interaction during sessions. The therapist remains fully responsible for clinical decisions, guidance, and session direction, while the system functions strictly as a controlled tool within the therapeutic process to increase awareness and support behavioral change
