1. Introduction

2. Nature of the Platform

Psychotherapy Now is an AI-assisted interface designed to support licensed clinicians in professional training, reflective exercises, and supervised clinical interactions.

The Platform is a tool. It is not a provider of psychotherapy or medical care and does not deliver treatment, diagnosis, or clinical services. All outputs generated by the Platform are automated, informational, and non-clinical in nature.

3. Eligibility

You may use the Platform only if you are a licensed mental health professional, provide accurate licensure and identity verification, and agree to these Terms. Use by unlicensed individuals or independent patient use is strictly prohibited.

4. Permitted Uses

4.1 Supervised Patient Use

You may use the Platform in connection with a patient only when you are present in real time and maintain full control of the interaction.

4.2 Professional Training

You may use the Platform for skill development, technique exploration, and simulation of clinical scenarios.

4.3 Self-Reflective Use

You may use the Platform for self-reflective professional exercises, subject to the terms of Section 5.

5. Self-Reflective Use and Role-Play

You may use the Platform in a self-directed manner, including role-playing as a patient and prompting the AI to respond as a therapist.

5.1 Definition

All such use is defined as self-directed reflective simulation. It is not therapy, treatment, or clinical care.

5.2 No Therapeutic Relationship

Use of the Platform does not create a therapist-patient relationship, a clinical relationship, or any duty of care.

5.3 Your Responsibility

You agree that you are not receiving therapy from the Platform, that you remain fully responsible for your own well-being, and that you are responsible for how you interpret and use all outputs.

5.4 Not a Substitute for Care

The Platform is not a substitute for psychotherapy, supervision, consultation, or medical or mental health treatment.

6. No Clinical Services Provided

The Platform does not diagnose, treat, assess risk, or provide clinical recommendations. All outputs are generated by an artificial system and do not constitute professional advice.

7. Use With Patients
